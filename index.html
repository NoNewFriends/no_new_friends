Sample Sundays - Popsicle Edition

3pm - 5pm Sunday, April 26th @
the Backyard of 2642 Bayou Road

In the backyard, on the front stoop, or in the middle of the neutral ground, Sample Sundays will be celebrations of our two favorite things- music and eats. Listen to DJ Backdrop and DJ CAX bump jams in that Sunday shine while savoring your favorite throwback snack and of course, making new friends.

For our first edition, we will be poppin’ popsicles. No better way to embrace the dog days of summer then kicking back with good people, good music, and some damn good popsicles. Music starts at 3pm.

NO New Friends Round Up

7:30, Friday September 19th 2015 @
Handsome Willy's

Want to expand your circle of friends? Looking for a date? Let No New Friends and the fine people at Handsome Willy's help! .Part music trivia, a little double dare, some speed dating, and part plain ol' good time, NO New Friends promises a positive and creative space for people from all over New Orleans to connect.
